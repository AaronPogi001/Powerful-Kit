Welcome to Connection Department

The Connection Department is a vital arm of the church focused on fostering meaningful relationships and ensuring that every individual who walks through the doors feels welcomed, supported, and integrated into the life of the church.This ministry is dedicated to building a strong sense of community by connecting people to the church's resources, groups, and oppurtunities for spiritural growth.

Welcome to Connection Department

It serves as the bridge between newcomers, regular attendees, and the broader church community, ensuring that everyone has the oppurtunity to engage, belong, and thrive within the church environment. Through intentional follow-up, ongoing support, and the creation of welcoming spaces, the Connection Department/Ministry plays a key role in helping individuals move from initial contact to active participation and dicipleship.

Welcome to Connection Department

This team handles the initial welcome, collects contact information, and ensures that follow-up and follow-through processes are in place to help new attendees integrate. They also help people take their next steps in faith, such as joining a small group, getting baptized, or finding a place to serve. The Connection Team ensures that no one is left out and that everyone has the opportunity to connect deeply with the church community.
