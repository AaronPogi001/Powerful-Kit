WHO WE ARE


The Connection Ministry is a vital arm of the church focused of fostering meaningful relationships and ensuring that every individual who walks through the doors feels welcomed, supported, and integrated into the life of the church. This ministry dedicated to building a strong sense of community by connecting people to the church's resources, groups, and oppurtunities for spiritual growth.It serves as the bridge between newcomers, regular attendees, and the broader church community, ensuring that everyone has the oppurtunity to engage, belong, and thrive within the church environment. Through intentional follow-up, ongoing support, and the creation of welcoming spaces, the Connection Department/Ministry plays a key role in helping individuals move from initial contact to active participation and dicipleship.

The Connection Team is responsible for helping church attendees build meaningful relationships within the church community. Their primary mission is to ensure that everyone who steps into the church feels welcomed and supported. This team handles the initial welcome, gathers contact information, and ensures that follow-up and follow-through processes are in place to integrate new attendees. They also provide assistance during altar calls and help individuals take their next steps in faith, whether that be joining a small group, getting baptised, or finding a place to serve. The Connection Team ensures that no one falls through the cracks and that everyone has the opportunity to connect deeply with the church community.
